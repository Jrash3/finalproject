Accessibility

  • Accessibility ensures websites can be used by everyone, including people with disabilities.
  • Readable text, proper color contrast, and easy navigation are essential for accessibility.
  • Tools like contrast checkers help developers confirm their site meets accessibility standards.

Helpful Accessibility Tool

WebAIM Contrast Checker
The accessibility checker is something that is used for every website. People with disabilities or just poor eyesight require websites to meet contrast standards for usability. This allows the user to determine if the site is accessible toward the majority of people.
